Happy Health

The project "Happy Health" is a group project. Our coach gave us a mock-up that we had to intergrate. The purpose of the site is to take data from open sources and show it via interactive boxes. The data that we used was health and happiness scores by country from 2015 to our days. We also had to implement random advices for life in another box at the bottom. These advices could be integrated via API from some site.
